Reasons you should own a pet

Companionship

Pets provide affection and interactions, reducing feelings of loneliness Pets offer comfort, emphathy and validation. Pets are always available for interaction, allevating isolation. Also, pets participate in activities, creating memories and strengthening bonds.

Routine

Feeding, walking, and caring for pets createes a daily routine. Pets thrive on routine, providing a sense of stability and normalcy. Caring for pets teaches prioritization and time allocation. Regular pet care activities promote consistency and reliability.

Stress Relief

Interacting with pets lowers cortisol levels, heart rate, and blood pressure. Also, playing with pets releases endorphins, improving mood and reducing anxiety. Caring for pets diverts attention from stressful thoughts and emotions. Petting or cuddling pets promotes relaxation and calmness.

Physical Activity

Walking, running or playing with pets encourages regular physical activity. Pets encourage exploration and outdoor exploration. Caring for pets especially dogs, promotes mobility and flexibility. Regular physical activity with pets reduces obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular risks.
